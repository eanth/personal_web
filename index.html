About Me
   What is this?

This is my personal website that I created to have fun and to try doing some neat things with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

This website also serves as my portfolio and contains links to my socials such as LinkedIn, Github, and LeetCode along with a copy of my resume if any potential employers stop by and like what they see.

I have built this site from the ground up so my apologies if there is any roughness around it's edges but it is a passion project and will constantly be improved. If you have any suggestions for improvement or notice any issues, please feel free to reach out be it here or any of my socials.

I want this site to serve as a reflection of me and as such I aimed to make it both fun and professional, so when you get bored of reading my resume and checking out my projects, if you still have a minute, feel free to play some free shareware/freeware DOS games in your browser such as Doom, Duke Nukem, X-Men and many more!

I hope you enjoy exploring it as much as I did making it.
